## Step 2: Connect Social Media Accounts

### 2.1 Navigate to Social Accounts

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select "Settings"
3. Click "Social Accounts" in the sidebar

### 2.2 Connect Instagram

**Requirements:**
- Instagram Business or Creator account
- Connected to a Facebook Page

**Steps:**
1. Click "Connect Account" > "Instagram"
2. Log in to Facebook
3. Select the Facebook Page linked to your Instagram
4. Grant permissions
5. Your Instagram account will appear in the list

**Troubleshooting:**
- Convert to Business account: Instagram Settings > Account > Switch to Professional Account
- Link to Facebook Page: Instagram Settings > Account > Linked Accounts > Facebook

### 2.3 Connect Facebook

**Steps:**
1. Click "Connect Account" > "Facebook"
2. Log in to Facebook
3. Select the Pages you want to manage
4. Grant permissions
5. Your Facebook Pages will appear in the list

**Note**: You can connect multiple Facebook Pages.

### 2.4 Connect Twitter/X

**Steps:**
1. Click "Connect Account" > "Twitter"
2. Log in to Twitter
3. Authorize the app
4. Your Twitter account will appear in the list

### 2.5 Connect LinkedIn

**Options:**
- Personal Profile
- Company Page (requires admin access)

**Steps:**
1. Click "Connect Account" > "LinkedIn"
2. Choose Personal or Company
3. Log in to LinkedIn
4. Grant permissions
5. Your LinkedIn account will appear in the list

### 2.6 Connect Other Platforms

Follow similar steps for:
- **TikTok**: Requires TikTok Business account
- **YouTube**: Requires channel ownership
- **Pinterest**: Requires Business account
- **Threads**: Linked through Instagram
- **Reddit**: Requires moderator access for subreddits

**Pro Tip**: Connect all your accounts now to save time later. You can always add more later.

## Step 3: Set Up Your Workspace

### 3.1 Workspace Information

1. Go to Settings > General
2. Fill in:
   - **Workspace Name**: Your brand or company name
   - **Industry**: Select your industry
   - **Timezone**: Your local timezone
   - **Language**: Primary language

### 3.2 Brand Information

1. Go to Settings > Branding
2. Upload:
   - **Logo**: Your brand logo (PNG, 500x500px recommended)
   - **Brand Colors**: Primary and secondary colors
   - **Brand Guidelines**: Upload brand guidelines document (optional)

### 3.3 Notification Preferences

1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Configure:
   - **Email Notifications**: Choose what you want to receive
   - **Push Notifications**: Enable for mobile app
   - **SMS Alerts**: For critical issues (optional)
   - **Slack Integration**: Connect your Slack workspace (optional)

**Recommended Settings:**
- ‚úÖ Post published successfully
- ‚úÖ Post failed to publish
- ‚úÖ New message in inbox
- ‚úÖ Approval request
- ‚úÖ Crisis alert
- ‚ùå Daily summary (can be overwhelming initially)

### 3.4 Team Members (Optional)

If you're working with a team:

1. Go to Settings > Team
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email address
4. Select role:
   - **Admin**: Full access
   - **Manager**: Can publish
   - **Editor**: Needs approval
   - **Viewer**: Read-only
5. Click "Send Invitation"

**Pro Tip**: Start with fewer permissions and increase as needed.

## Step 5: Create Your First Post

### 5.1 Open Post Editor

1. Click "Create Post" button (top right)
2. Or go to Content Calendar > Click any date > "Create Post"

### 5.2 Write Your Content

**Option A: Write Manually**
1. Type your content in the editor
2. Use the formatting toolbar
3. Add emojis with the emoji picker

**Option B: Use AI**
1. Click "AI Assist" button
2. Enter a topic or prompt (e.g., "Announce our new product launch")
3. Select tone (Professional, Casual, Friendly, etc.)
4. Click "Generate"
5. Review 3-5 variations
6. Select one and edit as needed

### 5.3 Add Media

1. Click "Add Media" button
2. Choose:
   - **Upload**: From your computer
   - **Media Library**: Previously uploaded files
   - **Stock Photos**: Unsplash/Pexels integration
   - **Canva**: Create design in Canva

**Supported Formats:**
- Images: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP (max 10MB)
- Videos: MP4, MOV (max 500MB)

**Pro Tip**: Use high-quality images (1080x1080px for Instagram).

### 5.4 Select Platforms

1. Check the platforms you want to post to
2. Click "Customize per platform" if you want different content for each

**Platform-Specific Tips:**
- **Instagram**: Use 10-15 hashtags, add first comment
- **Twitter**: Keep it concise, use 1-2 hashtags
- **LinkedIn**: Professional tone, 3-5 hashtags
- **Facebook**: Longer content works well

### 5.5 Add Hashtags

**Option A: Manual**
1. Type hashtags in your content (e.g., #marketing)

**Option B: AI Suggestions**
1. Click "Generate Hashtags"
2. Review 30 suggested hashtags
3. Select relevant ones
4. Click "Add to Post"

### 5.6 Preview Your Post

1. Click "Preview" button
2. See how your post looks on each platform
3. Make adjustments if needed

### 5.7 Publish or Schedule

**Publish Now:**
1. Click "Publish Now"
2. Confirm platforms
3. Click "Publish"
4. Your post will be published immediately

**Schedule for Later:**
1. Click "Schedule"
2. Select date and time
3. Or click "Optimal Time" for AI recommendation
4. Click "Schedule Post"

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Connect Social Account?
- Verify account credentials
- Check platform permissions
- Try different browser
- Clear cookies and cache
- Contact support if persists

### Post Failed to Publish?
- Check account connection
- Verify content meets platform guidelines
- Check media file size and format
- Review error message
- Try manual retry

### AI Not Generating Content?
- Check AI generation limit
- Verify subscription includes AI
- Make prompt more specific
- Train brand voice
- Contact support

### Analytics Not Showing?
- Wait for hourly update
- Check account connection
- Verify account has public data
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support
