## Troubleshooting

### Why can't I connect my Instagram account?

**Common Issues:**
- Using personal account → Convert to Business/Creator
- Not admin of Facebook Page → Get admin access
- App permissions revoked → Re-authorize
- Instagram API issues → Check status.platform.com

### Posts are not publishing

**Check:**
- Social account is connected
- Content meets platform guidelines
- Media files are correct format/size
- Not exceeding platform rate limits
- Scheduled time is in the future

### Analytics are not updating

**Solutions:**
- Wait for hourly update cycle
- Reconnect social account
- Check account has public data
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if persists

### AI is not generating content

**Verify:**
- Not exceeded monthly AI limit
- Prompt is clear and specific
- Brand voice is trained
- Subscription includes AI features
- No service outages

### Can't invite team members

**Possible Causes:**
- Reached user limit for plan
- Invalid email address
- Email already in use
- Not enough permissions
- Email in spam folder

## Best Practices

### How often should I post?

**Recommended Frequency:**
- **Instagram**: 3-7 times per week
- **Facebook**: 3-5 times per week
- **Twitter**: 3-5 times per day
- **LinkedIn**: 2-5 times per week
- **TikTok**: 1-3 times per day

Use our AI recommendations for your specific audience.

### What's the best time to post?

Varies by audience! Our AI analyzes your followers' activity to recommend optimal times. Generally:
- **B2B**: Weekdays 8am-10am, 12pm-2pm
- **B2C**: Evenings and weekends
- **Global**: Consider multiple time zones

### How do I grow my following?

**Proven Strategies:**
- Post consistently
- Use relevant hashtags
- Engage with your audience
- Share valuable content
- Collaborate with influencers
- Run contests and giveaways
- Use AI to optimize content

### Should I use hashtags?

Yes! But strategically:
- **Instagram**: 10-15 hashtags (mix of reach levels)
- **Twitter**: 1-2 hashtags
- **LinkedIn**: 3-5 hashtags
- **TikTok**: 3-5 hashtags

Use our AI hashtag generator for best results.

### How do I handle negative comments?

**Best Practices:**
- Respond quickly (within 1 hour)
- Stay professional and empathetic
- Take conversations private when needed
- Use our sentiment analysis to prioritize
- Set up crisis alerts for serious issues
