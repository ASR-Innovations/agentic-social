## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the platform homepage and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
4. Complete your profile setup with workspace name and preferences

### Connecting Social Media Accounts

1. Navigate to **Settings > Social Accounts**
2. Click **Connect Account** and select your platform
3. Authorize the platform connection through OAuth
4. Your account will appear in the connected accounts list
5. Repeat for all platforms you want to manage

**Supported Platforms:**
- Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Threads, Reddit, Google My Business

### Workspace Setup

Configure your workspace:
- **Workspace Name**: Your brand or company name
- **Timezone**: Set your local timezone for accurate scheduling
- **Brand Voice**: Train AI on your brand's tone and style
- **Team Members**: Invite collaborators with appropriate roles
- **Notification Preferences**: Choose how you want to be alerted

## Content Creation & Publishing

### Creating a New Post

1. Click **Create Post** from the dashboard or content calendar
2. Enter your content in the text editor
3. Add media by dragging and dropping or clicking upload
4. Select target platforms
5. Customize content for each platform if needed
6. Add hashtags, mentions, and links
7. Choose to publish immediately or schedule for later

### AI-Assisted Content Creation

Click **AI Assist** to generate content variations, optimize existing content, get hashtag suggestions, improve readability, and translate content.

## AI Hub

The AI Hub is your command center for AI-powered content operations.

### Content Generation
- Enter a topic or prompt
- Select tone (Professional, Casual, Friendly, Formal, Humorous)
- Choose target platforms
- Review 3-5 content variations
- Edit and customize as needed

### Hashtag Generator
- Analyzes your content automatically
- Suggests 30 hashtags categorized by reach (High, Medium, Niche)
- Shows estimated reach and competition level
- Save hashtag groups for reuse

### Brand Voice Training
1. Navigate to **AI Hub > Brand Voice**
2. Upload 5-10 examples of your best content
3. Click **Train Brand Voice**
4. AI analyzes tone, style, and vocabulary

### Automation Settings
- **Full Autonomous**: AI generates, schedules, and publishes automatically
- **Assisted**: AI generates content for your review
- **Manual**: AI provides suggestions only
- **Hybrid**: Mix of autonomous and manual

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Post failed to publish?**
- Check social media token (reconnect if expired)
- Verify platform API limits
- Review content for policy violations
- Check media file size

**Analytics not updating?**
- Analytics update hourly
- Check account connection status
- Verify account permissions

**Need help?**
- Help Center: Searchable knowledge base
- Live Chat: 24/7 for Pro/Enterprise
- Email: support@platform.com
