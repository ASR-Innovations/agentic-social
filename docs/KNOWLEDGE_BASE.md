## Account Management

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit the platform homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email through the confirmation link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile setup

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to Settings > Profile > Email Address and update it. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent and will delete all your data.

## Social Media Connections

### Which platforms are supported?

We support:
- Instagram (Personal & Business accounts)
- Facebook (Pages & Groups)
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn (Personal & Company pages)
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Pinterest
- Threads
- Reddit
- Google My Business
- WhatsApp Business

### How do I connect a social media account?

1. Go to Settings > Social Accounts
2. Click "Connect Account"
3. Select the platform you want to connect
4. Authorize the connection through OAuth
5. Your account will appear in the connected accounts list

### Why did my social account disconnect?

Common reasons:
- **Token expired**: Reconnect the account
- **Password changed**: Reconnect with new credentials
- **Permissions revoked**: Re-authorize the app
- **Platform API changes**: Update may be required

To reconnect: Go to Settings > Social Accounts > Click "Reconnect" next to the disconnected account.

### Can I connect multiple accounts from the same platform?

Yes! You can connect unlimited accounts from each platform (subject to your subscription plan limits).

### How do I remove a connected account?

1. Go to Settings > Social Accounts
2. Find the account you want to remove
3. Click the three dots menu
4. Select "Disconnect"
5. Confirm the action

## Content Creation

### How do I create a post?

1. Click "Create Post" from the dashboard or content calendar
2. Write your content in the text editor
3. Add media (images, videos, GIFs)
4. Select target platforms
5. Customize content for each platform if needed
6. Add hashtags and mentions
7. Choose to publish now or schedule for later

### What file formats are supported for media?

**Images:**
- JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Max size: 10MB per image
- Recommended: 1080x1080px for Instagram, 1200x628px for Facebook

**Videos:**
- MP4, MOV, AVI
- Max size: 500MB
- Max duration: 60 minutes
- Recommended: 1080p resolution

### Can I edit a scheduled post?

Yes! Go to the Content Calendar, click on the scheduled post, make your changes, and save. The post will be updated.

### How do I create platform-specific content?

When creating a post:
1. Select multiple platforms
2. Click "Customize per platform"
3. Edit content, hashtags, and media for each platform individually
4. Save your customizations

### Can I save drafts?

Yes! When creating a post, click "Save as Draft" instead of scheduling or publishing. Access your drafts from the Content Calendar > Drafts tab.

## AI Features

### How does AI content generation work?

Our AI analyzes your brand voice, target audience, and content goals to generate optimized posts. Simply:
1. Go to AI Hub
2. Enter a topic or prompt
3. Select tone and platforms
4. Review 3-5 generated variations
5. Edit and customize as needed

### What is Brand Voice Training?

Brand Voice Training teaches the AI your unique writing style:
1. Upload 5-10 examples of your best content
2. AI analyzes tone, vocabulary, and style
3. Future content matches your brand voice automatically

### How accurate is the AI?

Our AI uses GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet, achieving 95%+ accuracy. However, we always recommend reviewing and editing AI-generated content before publishing.

### Can I control how much AI is used?

Yes! Choose from four automation modes:
- **Full Autonomous**: AI generates, schedules, and publishes automatically
- **Assisted**: AI generates content for your review
- **Manual**: AI provides suggestions only
- **Hybrid**: Mix of autonomous and manual

### How much does AI cost?

AI usage is included in your subscription plan with generous limits:
- **Starter**: 100 AI generations/month
- **Professional**: 500 AI generations/month
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited AI generations

## Scheduling & Publishing

### How do I schedule a post?

1. Create your post
2. Click "Schedule"
3. Select date and time
4. Choose timezone
5. Click "Schedule Post"

### What is optimal posting time?

Our AI analyzes your audience activity over 90 days to recommend the best times to post for maximum engagement. Look for the "Optimal Time" suggestion when scheduling.

### Can I schedule recurring posts?

Yes! When scheduling:
1. Enable "Recurring Post"
2. Choose frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
3. Set end date or number of occurrences
4. Save

### What happens if a post fails to publish?

The system automatically retries 3 times with exponential backoff. If it still fails:
- You'll receive an email notification
- The post status shows "Failed" with error details
- You can manually retry or reschedule

### Can I bulk schedule posts?

Yes! Use CSV upload:
1. Go to Content Calendar > Bulk Upload
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in your posts
4. Upload the file
5. Review and confirm

## Social Listening

### What is social listening?

Social listening monitors mentions of your brand, keywords, and competitors across social media and the web in real-time.

### How do I set up listening queries?

1. Go to Listening > Queries
2. Click "Create Query"
3. Enter keywords, hashtags, or @mentions
4. Select platforms to monitor
5. Set up alerts
6. Save

### What is sentiment analysis?

AI analyzes the emotional tone of mentions, categorizing them as:
- **Positive**: Praise, satisfaction, enthusiasm
- **Neutral**: Factual, informational
- **Negative**: Complaints, criticism, dissatisfaction

### How do crisis alerts work?

Crisis detection monitors for:
- Sudden sentiment drops
- Volume spikes in negative mentions
- Specific crisis keywords

When detected, you receive immediate alerts via SMS, email, and push notifications.

### Can I track competitors?

Yes! Add competitor accounts to monitor their:
- Posting frequency
- Content types
- Engagement rates
- Audience growth
- Campaign launches

## Troubleshooting

### Post failed to publish

**Possible causes:**
- Social account disconnected → Reconnect account
- Content violates platform policies → Review and edit
- Media file too large → Compress or resize
- API rate limit reached → Wait and retry

### Analytics not showing

**Solutions:**
- Wait for hourly update cycle
- Check account connection status
- Verify account has public data
- Clear browser cache

### Can't connect social account

**Try:**
- Use correct account credentials
- Check platform is not experiencing outages
- Clear browser cookies
- Try different browser
- Ensure account has necessary permissions

### AI not generating content

**Check:**
- AI generation limit not exceeded
- Prompt is clear and specific
- Brand voice is trained
- Subscription plan includes AI

### Slow performance

**Optimize:**
- Clear browser cache
- Close unnecessary tabs
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Contact support if persists
